About Orderly

Orderly is an app that will allow you keep track of your personal medical needs. It is useful for you to chat with your doctor, attend online video appointments for non threatening medical issues, receive updates on your visits, schedule appointments, even visit your physician in a socially distant way.


Created because you should be able to talk to all of your doctors view all of your information, no matter how many you may have; all in one app, Orderly

Services.

Schedule Appointments:

With the click of a few buttons. You can set an appointment straight from your pocket. Online, in person, It automatically syncs with your calender or reminders to ensure you are always aware of when you visit your doctor next.

Online Chat:

When you wake up in the morning with a sore throat. No one wants to leave work early, instead of taking the time to make a trip. Chat with your doctor online!

Wireless Check In:

For those trips you take to the Doctors office, all the paper work and sign in sheets are a drag. Avoid getting sick because you forgot to sanitize after touching the pen because you forgot your own. Orderly features a qr check in. Specific for your doctor. Specific for you.
